Read,Share and explore!

BookStation allows users to borrow, lend, swap or sell their books, from the readers and to the readers !

Continue To The Web Version!

What?

Bookstation is an online community that allows readers and book owners to exchange books in many diiferent ways , each user can borrow , swap or even buy the book he wants from other users , libraries are awesome , and that's why we decide to combine all the books users own into a one huge library!

Why?

We believe that anyone should be able to read the books he wants whenever he wants and wherever he wants, and we also believe in authors ' and publishers' rights, so we figure that there must be a sweet spot between paying hundreds of pounds for abook and downloading and illegal PDF copy of it , and that exactly what we are doing!, exactly like a library, but without all the limitations and restrictions, we are trying to create THE ulitmate library, from the readers and to the readers!

How?

All you have to do is to create an account(you can easily register using your Google account),list the books you own and their availability (for lend, swap... etc)and start searching and exploring all the books that other people have listed, when you find and intersting book, you simply send a request to its owner, and when he accepts, you view each other contacts to schedule a meeting and that's it!
We highly recommend linking a goodreads account to make the whole process much easier.
